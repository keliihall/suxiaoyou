# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in the 苏小有 community a
welcoming, safe, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of
background, identity, experience level, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent while remaining open to correction.
- Protect private information and respect reasonable boundaries.
- Accept moderation decisions and help keep discussions relevant.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, deliberate
intimidation, doxxing, sustained disruption, and other conduct that a
reasonable person would consider hostile or unsafe are not acceptable.

## Scope and enforcement

This policy applies in the repository, issue tracker, pull requests,
discussions, release channels, and public spaces where someone represents the
project. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close or lock discussions,
reject contributions, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

Report conduct concerns to the repository owner through the contact method
listed on the [keliihall GitHub profile](https://github.com/keliihall). Include
only the information needed to investigate. Reports will be handled as
confidentially as reasonably possible; retaliation against reporters is not
permitted.
